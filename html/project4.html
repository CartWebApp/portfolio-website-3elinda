Budget Calculator

Category

Financial

Corporate Website

This budget calculator is a simple tool developed to help users track their income and expenses and manage their savings. It features a visually appealing color scheme, a user-friendly layout, and customizable categories. Built with the purpose of making budget calculations easier by inputting financial information. Users are able to manage their monthly income and expenses, saving themselves the time and struggle of doing it themselves. They are also able to overview their expenses breakdown.

Features

Spending Breakdown

Balance

Simple Navigation

Pleasant Color Scheme

Fast Loading Speed

Readable Text

Basic Structure in Place

Taxes Done Dynamically

Work in Progress

Goal

Create a website that makes the process of calculating a budget easier for people.

While the calculator serves as a basis foundation, future updates will include enhancing functionality, improving accessibility, visually appealing breakdown such as a pie chart, printable summary, better responsiveness, enhanced usability, and overall better user experience.
