Beaded Jewelry

Category

Personal Project

After a family vacation to Mexico years ago I discovered the art of making beaded bracelets, earrings, and necklaces. I started with the purpose of doing it as a hobby, simply because I liked the process of creating things with my own hands but it later developed into a small business. Jewelry was then made with the goal of making my customers happy and making something that they would want to wear everyday.

Goal

Create beaded jewelry that enables people to express themselves.

While this small business served as a structure for something possible I want to do in the future, improvements include making keychains, developing different designs, and expanding the types of jewelry to not just beads.
